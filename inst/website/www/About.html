About

This website was developed at the department of Haemotology, Aalborg University Hospital.

If you encounter any issues or bugs using either the hemaClass website or the hemaClass R-package, or if you have suggestions for improvements please do not hesitate to report it to us at GitHub. You are also very welcome to send us questions or comments via e-mail.

Note: The website does not support uploading .CEL files in Internet Explorer 9 and older. Please, use a newer or different browser.

For more information about our group go to www.blodet.dk.
