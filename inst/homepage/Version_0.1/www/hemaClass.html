Welcome to hemaClass

This homepage is dedicated to GEP based classification of DLBCL", "patients according to various classifications algorithms. The server currently supports gene expressions profiled by the Affymetrix GeneChip Human Genome U133 Plus 2.0 Array.

Functionality

The current classification schemes build are:

  • Cell of origin classifiers
    • BAGS: B-cell associated signatures
    • ABC/GCB; Activated B-cell like or Germinal center B-cell like
  • Resistance Gene Signatures (REGS)
    • Rituximab (R)
    • Cyclophosphamide (C)
    • Doxorubicin (H)
    • Vincristine (O)
    • Dexamethasone (P)
    • Melphalan (M)
