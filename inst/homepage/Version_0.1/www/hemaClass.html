GEP Based Classification of DLBCL Patients

This homepage is dedicated to classification of DLBCL patients according various classifications algorithms.

Functionality

The current classification schemes build are:

  • Cell of origin classifiers
    • BAGS: B-cell associated signatures
    • ABC/GCB; Activated B-cell like or Germinal center B-cell like
  • Resistance Gene Signatures (REGS)
    • Rituximab (R)
    • Cyclophosphamide (C)
    • Doxorubicin (H)
    • Vincristine (O)
    • Dexamethasone (P)
    • Melphalan (M)
